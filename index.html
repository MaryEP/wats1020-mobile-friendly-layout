Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide quality care for all genus and species of arachnoids in this country and beyond.

To inspire breakthroughs in the way the world treats spiders and to achieve immediate and lasting change in their lives.

The Spider Hospital - Rules for the Care of Spiders

Ten Rules for the Care of Spiders

Rule 1: If a spider has a broken leg, use very tiny tape to form a cast.

Rule 2: Give spiders lots of rest and plenty of water.

Rule 3: Spiders don't swim, so help them out of water.

Rule 4: Watch for spiders near the shower drain.

Rule 5: Move a spider up to the lampshade for safety.

Rule 6: Look for spiders before lighting a fire.

Rule 7: Don't put a spider in the oven.

Rule 8: Look inside the tea kettle before turning on the stove.

Rule 9: Use a cotton ball if a spider is hurt.

Rule 10: Don't paint a spider.
